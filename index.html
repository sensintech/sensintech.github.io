  • What We Do

    Wireless Localization And Navigation Systems

    SIT: Seamless Integrated Indoor and Outdoor Localization

    Mission: Integration + Coherence + Analytics

    SIT bridges the gap between indoor and outdoor missions. Together with GPS, LPWAN and 4G/5G techniques, SIT brings seamless integrated experiences.

    WinIPS: WiFi-based Non-intrusive Indoor Positioning System

    Mission: Simplicity+ Accuracy + Analytics

    WinIPS brings nearly ZERO infrastructure cost (reuse of existing WiFi infrastructures) with app-less mobile devices (MDs)

    IIN: IBeacon-based Indoor Navigation

    Mission: Precision+ Attractiveness+ Analytics

    Our indoor localization and navigation system empowers LBS with the information, resources and analytics based on WiFi, Bluetooth, RFID, UWB. It helped drive up customers' engagement by up to 300 percent.

    RIPS: RFID based Indoor Positioning System

    Mission: Bargain + Accuracy + Analytics

    RIPS tracks cheap RFID devices and provides bargain solutions

  • Who We Are

    We're on a mission to connect your cause to the people who care the most.

    XIE Lihua

    Technical Consultant

    Dr Xie is currently a Professor in Singapore. He was a Changjiang visiting professor of South China University of Technology from 2006 to 2010. He is currently an Editor-in- Chief of Unmanned Systems and an Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Control System Technology and Sciences in China. is a Fellow of IEEE, Fellow of IFAC and IEEE Distinguished Lecturer. He was an appointed member of Board of Governors, IEEE Control Systems Society, 2011, and an elected member of Board of Governors, IEEE Control Systems Society from Jan. 2016 to Dec. 2018.

    JIANG Hao

    Chief Technology Officer

    Dr Jiang is currently a senior lecturer at Fuzhou University. He was a post-doctoral fellow at Nanyang Technological University from 2013-2015. His research focuses on indoor location and navigation systems. He was one of the main contributors for the project "Singapore-Berkeley Building Efficiency and Sustainability in the Tropics" in Berkeley Education Alliance for Research in Singapore

    ZOU Han

    Chief Architect

    Dr Zou is currently a visiting scholar at UC Berkeley. He obtained his PhD degree from Nanyang Technological University. He has published over 15 papers and 1 patent in localization technologies

    YANG Jianfei

    Chief Software Engineer

    Mr Yang is currently a PhD student at Nanyang Technological University. He has published 10 international papers and his research focuses on the integration of artificial intelligence and internet of things. He got champions in several challenging competitions including Microsoft Beauty of Programming Challenge 2016, Buildathon in Singapore’s Ministry of Education, and Chinese Internet of Everything Competition. He also got first prize in many discipline contests, such as National IOT Design Contest, National Mobile Internet Innovation Contest, Microsoft Imagine Cup in China and American Mathematical Contest in Modeling. He interned as a vision engineer in DJI in 2015 and has two years’ experience of start-up in e-commerce.

    XU Jun

    Chief Strategist

    Dr Xu has 16 years industrial and academic experiences and multi-discipline knowledge in complex system design, management, modeling and simulation, data analytics, IoT and cloud storage. He has published around 60 international papers and US patents and 1 monograph. He was a committee member of several international conferences on control and automation, and is an editor of the journal unmanned systems. He is a senior member of IEEE and a certificated FRM.
